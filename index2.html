ASCII Art Decoder:
Custom Mask, Mapping, Morse, Numeric, A-Z, Base64, Auto Bar Mask

Only columns at these positions (across) are extracted from each line, then joined together. Example: 2 4 5 7 extracts the 2nd, 4th, 5th, and 7th character from each line.
Auto Bar Mask scans your ASCII art and selects all column positions containing a bar ( | ) on any line.
Use = to map input to output: e.g., O=A, .=., *=X
Multi-char matches allowed (e.g. ||=M, |=I).
